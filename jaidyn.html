This look’s success relied on two items: hot glue and a loose shimmery powder. After completing the base makeup, I used a hot glue gun to create tear drop shapes on a metal sheet. When they dried completely, I dusted each tear with the insert powder here, peeled them off the metal sheet, and glued them on the face using lash glue. Since this look is supposedly ‘wet’ from the tears, I also used products that would emulate a dewy effect, such as the NYX Dewy setting spray, and the Fenty Beauty Gloss Bomb Ice lipgloss in the colour “Cold Heart’d.
