It is no surprise that color is a vital aspect of makeup. It creates visual interest and brings the face to life. In the name of unconventionalism, I took inspiration from greyscale printing to create a look with no color. To challenge the existence of colour in cosmetic work and test my colour theory knowledge, I mixed black and white face paints in varying ratios to create highlights and contours for this look. Pro tip: mixing black and white creates a blue-ish grey, so add some orange to neutralize the colour.
