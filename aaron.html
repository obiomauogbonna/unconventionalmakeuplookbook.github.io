Recently, I have noticed pearls are used frequently in various makeup styles, either in minimal quantities for spicing up simple looks or maximal amounts for a more expressive composition. For this makeup, I wanted to involve birth month stones and create a juxtaposition by constructing a gruesome concept with beautiful materials. Aaron's birthday falls in the month of June, so I decided to use pearls (June's declared gemstone) to mimic a moldy flesh-eating disease.
